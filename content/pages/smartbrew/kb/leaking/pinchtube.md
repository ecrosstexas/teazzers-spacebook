## Possible Cause

- Pinch tube leaking

## What to Check - Phone

- Pinch tube seated properly
- Valve handle tight

## Action Needed - Phone

- Educate the customer on how to do the following:
    1) Remove and clean tube with hot soapy water.
    2) Replace pinch tube.
    3) Remove and reset the tube.
